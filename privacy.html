Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sivil1 (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Vehicle Information

Sivil1 retrieves vehicle-related information using publicly available government APIs. This information may include vehicle specifications, registration-related data, and other publicly available attributes.

This data originates from official public sources and does not constitute private personal information.

1.2 Data Processed and Stored by the App

To improve performance and reliability, the App may process and temporarily store:

  • Vehicle data retrieved from public sources
  • Summarized or analyzed results generated by the App
  • Cached responses to reduce repeated external API requests

1.3 Personal Data and Device Information

Sivil1 does not collect, store, or associate personal data with individual users or their devices.

  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected
  • No user accounts are required
  • No persistent identifiers are stored to identify users
  • No personal data is tied to a specific device for tracking or profiling

Any data stored by the App is not linked to an identifiable individual or device.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide vehicle information requested by users
  • Generate summaries and analysis
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Prevent excessive or abusive API usage

3. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services that operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

  • Public government APIs
  • Advertising services (such as Google AdMob)
  • Analytics or crash reporting tools (if enabled)

These services may collect limited technical information required for their operation. We do not control how third-party services collect or use such data.

4. Advertising

If the App displays advertisements, they may be provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These providers may collect device-related or usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We do not store or associate advertising data with identifiable users or devices.

5. Data Retention

Cached or processed data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide functionality, improve performance, and maintain system stability.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

7. Children’s Privacy

Sivil1 is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
