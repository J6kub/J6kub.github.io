Technology Overview

Business Process Automation (BPA)

BPA involves the use of technology to automate complex business processes and workflows to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Examples: Automating invoice processing and approvals, streamlining customer onboarding processes, integrating CRM systems with marketing automation tools.

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

RPA involves the use of software robots or "bots" to automate repetitive, rule-based tasks usually performed by humans.

Examples: Automating data entry, processing transactions, extracting data from emails and entering it into a database.

Artificial Intelligence Automation (AIA)

AIA uses AI technologies such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision to automate tasks that require cognitive abilities.

Examples: Chatbots for customer service, automated fraud detection systems, predictive maintenance systems.
