Global Factory Overview

What is a Global Factory?

A global factory refers to the practice of producing goods in countries other than where the company is headquartered. This is typically done to take advantage of lower production costs, including cheaper labor and materials.

Pros and Cons of a Global Factory

Pros

  • Cost-effective production
  • Supports local economies
  • Creates job opportunities in developing countries

Cons

  • Ethical concerns
  • Risk of child labor
  • Job loss in the company's home country
