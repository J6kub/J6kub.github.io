Technology Overview

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

ERP systems are used for business management, combining fields like HR, finance, supply chain, logistics, and other parts of a business into one integrated system. They store information in a database and have analytic features such as forecasting based on sales and customer information.

Supply Chain Management (SCM)

SCM involves managing the supply chain by ensuring a good flow of information between businesses participating in the supply chain. This ensures efficient and effective flow of products and services. For example, the production business must always be well-connected with the supply and distribution businesses.

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

CRM focuses on managing customer data and devising strategies to attract and retain customers. The data gathered about customers is used to personalize coupons and advertisements to match their interests, thereby benefiting both the business and the customers.

Decision Support System (DSS)

DSS are analytical systems that help in making decisions or solving problems using statistical data and analysis. They have a narrow scope, focusing on specific, problem-specific, one-time decisions.

Business Intelligence (BI)

BI involves analyzing current market trends to expand the customer base and stay updated. It provides overall insights into business performance with ongoing and continuous analysis, helping businesses make informed decisions.
