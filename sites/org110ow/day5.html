Day 5: Organizational Change I

16. Why Organizations Change

Respond to environment, competition, technology, regulation, internal pressure.

17. Organizational Inertia

Resistance to change due to routines, politics, culture. Overcome by urgency, leadership, communication.

18. Perspectives of Change

  • Life cycle: Predictable stages
  • Teleological: Planned, purposeful

19. Signals of Needed Change

Declining performance, customer complaints, staff turnover, missed opportunities.

20. Perceptions Matter

Different stakeholders perceive problems differently. Key to align perceptions.
