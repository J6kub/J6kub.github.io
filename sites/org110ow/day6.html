Day 6: Organizational Change II & Digitalization

21. System Perspective on Change

Organizations are interconnected systems. One change affects others. Use feedback loops.

22. Limits to Change

Configurations/archetypes resist certain changes. Not all change paths are feasible.

23. Loose vs Tight Coupling

Loose: flexible, easier symbolic change. Tight: rigid, harder real change.

24. Slack Resources

Can aid change (room to experiment) or cause complacency.

25. Time in Change

Short vs long-term impact. Managing perceptions of time affects success.

26–33. Strategy E vs Strategy O

  • Strategy E: Economic, top-down, structure & financial results
  • Strategy O: Organizational capabilities, culture, participation

34–40. Digitalization & Disruption

  • Traits: Exponential, digital, combinatorial
  • Shifts: Machine > Mind, Product > Platform, Core > Crowd
  • Challenges for incumbents: Recognition, response, implementation
