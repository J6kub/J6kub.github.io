Day 4: Strategy, Culture & Motivation

11. Purpose of Strategy

Guides decisions and resource allocation. Leaders set direction, members implement and adjust it.

12. Two Strategic Paths

  • Low-cost: Operate efficiently (e.g., Walmart).
  • Differentiation: Offer unique value (e.g., Apple).

13. Importance of Culture

Defines identity, norms, and behavior. Manifests in rituals, symbols, language, office layout.

14. Changing Culture

Use leadership example, communicate vision, hire/promote aligned individuals, reinforce through systems.

15. Motivation Types

  • Intrinsic: Enjoyment, purpose (e.g., creative jobs).
  • Extrinsic: Pay, bonuses (e.g., sales roles).
